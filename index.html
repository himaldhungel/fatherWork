About Us

Bridging Farmers to Industries Since 2018

Based in Chandrapur, Rautahat, we are a leading crop aggregator dedicated to supporting local farmers while meeting the demands of Nepal's growing industrial sector.

Our mission is to create sustainable value chains that benefit both agricultural producers and industrial consumers, contributing to Nepal's food security and economic development.

  • Industrial Partners in Hetauda

  • Industrial Partners in Biratnagar
